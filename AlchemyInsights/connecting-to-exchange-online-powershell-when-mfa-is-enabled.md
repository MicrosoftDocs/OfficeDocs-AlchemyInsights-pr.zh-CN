# <a name="connect-to-exchange-online-powershell-when-mfa-is-enabled"></a>启用 MFA 时连接到 Exchange Online PowerShell

如果你的帐户启用了多重身份验证 (MFA), 则需要按照以下说明连接到 Exchange Online PowerShell:[使用多重身份验证连接到 Exchange Online powershell](https://docs.microsoft.com/powershell/exchange/exchange-online/connect-to-exchange-online-powershell/mfa-connect-to-exchange-online-powershell)。

**注意**: 即使你已使用[常规连接说明](https://docs.microsoft.com/powershell/exchange/exchange-online/connect-to-exchange-online-powershell/connect-to-exchange-online-powershell)连接到过去的 Exchange Online PowerShell, 你也需要在为帐户启用 mfa 后使用 mfa 连接说明。
