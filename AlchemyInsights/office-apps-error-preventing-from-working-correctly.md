- 验证 [或安装](https://support.office.com/article/update-office-and-your-computer-with-microsoft-update-2ab296f3-7f03-43a2-8e50-46de917611c5) Office 的最新更新

- [卸载](https://support.office.com/article/uninstall-office-from-a-pc-9dd49b83-264a-477a-8fcc-2fdf5dbf61d8) 并重新安装 Office
