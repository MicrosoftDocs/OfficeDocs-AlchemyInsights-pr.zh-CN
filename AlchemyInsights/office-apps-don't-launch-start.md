# <a name="microsoft-365-apps-crash-stop-responding-or-dont-launch"></a>Microsoft 365 应用崩溃、停止响应或不启动

若要修复 Microsoft 365 应用程序挂起或崩溃，请尝试以下选项：

- 调查为 Office 安装的[外接程序可能存在的问题](https://support.office.com/article/powerpoint-isn-t-responding-hangs-or-freezes-652ede6e-e3d2-449a-a07f-8c800dfb948d#bkmk_addins)
- 检查[防病毒软件](https://support.office.com/article/powerpoint-isn-t-responding-hangs-or-freezes-652ede6e-e3d2-449a-a07f-8c800dfb948d?ocmsassetID#bkmk_conflict)是否与 Microsoft 365 应用程序冲突
- 验证或[安装最新的 Office 更新](https://support.office.com/article/update-office-and-your-computer-with-microsoft-update-2ab296f3-7f03-43a2-8e50-46de917611c5)
- 使用 "控制面板"[修复 Office](https://support.office.com/article/repair-an-office-application-7821d4b6-7c1d-4205-aa0e-a6b40c5bb88b)
- [卸载](https://support.office.com/article/uninstall-office-from-a-pc-9dd49b83-264a-477a-8fcc-2fdf5dbf61d8)并重新安装 Office
