# <a name="add-update-or-remove-your-payment-methods"></a>添加、更新或删除付款方式

可以使用 **信用卡或借记卡或银行帐户** 从 Microsoft 购买产品和服务。 可在[付款方式](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=2018806)页面或进行购买时输入付款信息。

- [添加付款方式](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/manage-payment-methods#add-a-payment-method)

- [更新付款方式详细信息](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/manage-payment-methods#update-payment-method-details)

- [替换付款方式](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/manage-payment-methods#replace-a-payment-method)

- [删除付款方式](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/manage-payment-methods#delete-a-payment-method)

- [付款方式疑难解答](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/manage-payment-methods#troubleshoot-payment-methods)
